Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking an appointment for personalized consultations, especially for custom design services.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and offer flexible financing options for purchases over $1,000.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we provide insured international shipping to most countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all standard items. Custom pieces can be adjusted to ensure your satisfaction.
